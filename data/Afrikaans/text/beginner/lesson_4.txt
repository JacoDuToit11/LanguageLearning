Lesson 4: Food and Drink
In this lesson, we will learn basic vocabulary related to food and drinks, which is important when dining or shopping.

1. Water. Water. Water.  
2. Bread. Brood. Brood.  
3. Milk. Melk. Melk.  
4. Coffee. Koffie. Koffie.  
5. Tea. Tee. Tee.  
6. Juice. Sap. Sap.  
7. Fruit. Vrugte. Vrugte.  
8. Vegetables. Groente. Groente.  
9. Meat. Vleis. Vleis.  
10. Fish. Vis. Vis.  
11. Chicken. Hoender. Hoender.  
12. Rice. Rys. Rys.  
13. Pasta. Pasta. Pasta.  
14. Dessert. Nagereg. Nagereg.  
15. Sweet. Soet. Soet.  
16. Salty. Sout. Sout.  
17. Sour. Sure. Sure.  
18. I would like... Ek wil graag... Ek wil graag...  
19. Can I have...? Kan ek hê...? Kan ek hê...?  
20. It tastes good. Dit smaak lekker. Dit smaak lekker.  
21. It is delicious. Dit is heerlik. Dit is heerlik.  
22. I’m full. Ek is vol. Ek is vol.  
23. What do you want to eat? Wat wil jy eet? Wat wil jy eet?  
24. Let’s eat. Kom ons eet. Kom ons eet.  
25. Do you want some? Wil jy 'n bietjie hê? Wil jy 'n bietjie hê?
