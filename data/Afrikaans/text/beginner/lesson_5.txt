Lesson 5: Family and Relationships
In this lesson, we will talk about family members and relationships, which is important for sharing about yourself.

1. Mother. Ma. Ma.  
2. Father. Pa. Pa.  
3. Brother. Broer. Broer.  
4. Sister. Suster. Suster.  
5. Son. Seun. Seun.  
6. Daughter. Dogter. Dogter.  
7. Grandmother. Ouma. Ouma.  
8. Grandfather. Oupa. Oupa.  
9. Aunt. Tannie. Tannie.  
10. Uncle. Oom. Oom.  
11. Cousin (male). Neef. Neef.  
12. Cousin (female). Nies. Nies.  
13. Friend. Vriend. Vriend.  
14. Best friend. Beste vriend. Beste vriend.  
15. Partner. Vriend / Vriendin. Vriend / Vriendin.  
16. I love my family. Ek het my familie lief. Ek het my familie lief.  
17. We are close. Ons is naby. Ons is naby.  
18. I have a sister. Ek het 'n suster. Ek het 'n suster.  
19. Do you have siblings? Het jy broers of susters? Het jy broers of susters?  
20. My brother is younger. My broer is jonger. My broer is jonger.  
21. My sister is older. My suster is ouer. My suster is ouer.  
22. How many children do you have? Hoeveel kinders het jy? Hoeveel kinders het jy?  
23. I have one child. Ek het een kind. Ek het een kind.  
24. Are you married? Is jy getroud? Is jy getroud?  
25. Yes, I am married. Ja, ek is getroud. Ja, ek is getroud.
